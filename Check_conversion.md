# Conversion & Trust Checklist

## Above the Fold
- Value proposition clear within 5 seconds — answers "Is this for me?"
- Primary CTA visible without scrolling, action-oriented text (not "Submit" or "Click here")
- Phone number / contact visible in header on mobile (click-to-call)

## Trust Signals
- Customer testimonials or reviews with name + company (or initials if privacy-sensitive)
- Professional credentials displayed: Meisterbetrieb, HWK, Innungsmitglied, certifications
- Partner/client logos if permitted
- "Bekannt aus" / media mentions if applicable
- Google Reviews widget or rating badge if available

## CTAs & Conversion
- One primary CTA per page — clear visual hierarchy, contrasting color
- Secondary CTA as alternative (e.g. "Jetzt anrufen" vs. "Kontaktformular")
- CTA repeated at logical scroll points (after benefits, after testimonials, footer)
- Contact form: minimal fields (name, email/phone, message) — every extra field reduces conversion
- Thank-you/confirmation page or inline success message after form submit

## Content Strategy
- Benefits before features — lead with what the customer gains
- Use real photos over stock where possible — authenticity builds trust
- Service pages answer: what, for whom, why us, what's next
- Pricing transparency if applicable — even a "starting from" indicator helps

## Technical Trust
- SSL/HTTPS enforced (green padlock visible)
- Professional email domain (not @gmail.com in contact info)
- Fast load time signals competence — slow sites undermine credibility
- No broken links, no 404 pages, no placeholder text in production
